Madame Joyce Project

Digital Marketing Class Project · University of Northampton · Where I learned to pitch, persuade, and communicate strategy with impact.

Project
Madame Joyce Campaign
Type
MSc Class Project
Year
2025
Institution
University of Northampton

The Madame Joyce project challenged me to go beyond theory and apply real digital marketing thinking to an actual subject. I developed a marketing approach that could communicate a compelling story to both specialist and non-specialist audiences.

This meant thinking carefully about messaging, tone, visual presentation, and building a consistent brand narrative from scratch — pitching my strategy, defending creative decisions, and making people believe in my approach.

The Core Challenge

Communicate the same message in two completely different ways: once to a specialist marketing audience who would scrutinise the strategy, and once to a non-specialist audience who simply needed to feel connected to the brand. Getting both right required a deep understanding of audience psychology — one of the most valuable skills in digital marketing.

Skills Gained

✍️

Content Marketing

Crafting messages that inform, persuade, and engage across different audience types — adapting a core message without losing its essence.

🎨

Visual Communication

Using design and layout to reinforce brand identity and make information instantly understandable — especially vital in property marketing.

🔗

Brand Consistency

Every post, graphic, and caption must feel like it belongs to the same brand. Inconsistency erodes credibility — consistency builds trust.

How This Applies to Real Estate Marketing

SkillReal Estate Application
Content MarketingCreating property listings, blog content, and social media posts that attract buyers and build developer authority online.
Visual CommunicationProducing high-impact property visuals, virtual tour content, and brand materials that make listings stand out and feel premium.
Brand ConsistencyHelping real estate agencies maintain a unified brand voice across all platforms — from Instagram to Google Ads to email campaigns.
Pitching & PersuasionPresenting marketing strategies to property clients with clarity and confidence, earning their trust before the first campaign even launches.

The Art of the Pitch

In digital marketing, especially in real estate, you are always selling before you sell. Before a client signs a contract, before a buyer clicks a listing — someone has to win their confidence first. The Madame Joyce project taught me that pitching is not about talking louder. It is about listening carefully, understanding what matters to your audience, and presenting your case in a way that makes the decision easy.

"The best marketers don't just run campaigns — they win trust first."
